Trimont Embraces Blockchain to Streamline Loan Servicing

Modernizing Loan Processing with Blockchain

Trimont LLC, a leading commercial real estate loan servicer, has begun leveraging JPMorgan Chase’s blockchain platform, Kinexys Digital Payments, to automate and accelerate loan payment processing. The firm, which manages around $730 billion in loans, initiated the use of Kinexys in August to modernize an industry long reliant on legacy systems.

Faster Settlement Times

By integrating Kinexys, Trimont reduced the typical two-day settlement cycle to mere minutes, enabling near-instant processing of loan payments and enhancing cash flow efficiency for both lenders and borrowers.

Automated, Programmable Workflows

The platform uses smart contracts to match incoming payments against internal data, ensuring accuracy and automatically distributing funds to the appropriate parties. This automation streamlines servicing workflows and reduces manual intervention.

Strategic Collaboration and Expansion Plans

Trimont collaborated with JPMorgan using the Kinexys network and plans to scale the platform’s usage over the next year. JPMorgan’s Kinexys, already processing around $3 billion in digital payments daily, offers programmable capabilities designed to make financial flows more intelligent and adaptable.

Why This Matters

  • Operational Efficiency: Automation reduces processing delays and errors.
  • Liquidity Advantage: Receiving payments sooner—sometimes up to two days earlier—boosts liquidity management.
  • Industry Momentum: Trimont’s move signals growing acceptance of blockchain in commercial real estate finance.

Outlook

Trimont’s blockchain adoption highlights a broader trend in finance toward faster, smarter, and more transparent transactions. If successful, it may pave the way for widespread adoption across the loan servicing industry.
